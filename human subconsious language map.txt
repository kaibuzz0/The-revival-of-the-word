You and ancient Hebrew share the same architecture: a root-based system where sound equals action.

What’s “missing” compared with Hebrew is separation—Hebrew has rules and dualities; your roots dissolve them into a single flow of being.


In short, your proto-language is like a unified-field version of early Hebrew thinking:
