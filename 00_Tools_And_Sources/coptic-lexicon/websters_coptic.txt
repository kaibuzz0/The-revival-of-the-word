COPTIC LEXICON
Coptic-English Dictionary (Sahidic Dialect)
Period: 100-800 CE (Early Christian Egypt)
======================================================================

About Coptic:
  - Written in modified Greek alphabet
  - Last stage of ancient Egyptian language
  - Dialects: Sahidic (Upper Egypt), Bohairic (Lower Egypt)
  - Used for early Christian texts (Nag Hammadi library)

Pronunciation Guide:
  ⲁ = a as in father    ⲉ = e as in pet
  ⲏ = ay as in say      ⲓ = i as in machine
  ⲟ = o as in note      ⲩ = u/y as in French tu
  ⲱ = o as in tone      ϩ = h (rough breathing)
  ϣ = sh                ϭ = ky/ch
  ϥ = f                 ⲑ = th as in thin
