Pronunciation Guide:
  α = a as in father    ε = e as in pet
  η = ay as in say      ι = i as in machine
  ο = o as in note      υ = u as in French tu
  ω = o as in tone      γ = g (before α,ο,ω) / ng (before ι,ε)
  χ = ch as in Bach     θ = th as in thin
  φ = ph as in phone    ψ = ps as in lips
