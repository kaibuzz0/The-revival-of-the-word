======================================================================
GE'EZ (GƏʿƏZ) LEXICON
Classical Ethiopic Dictionary
Liturgical Language of Ethiopian Christianity
======================================================================

About Ge'ez:
  - Ancient South Semitic language
  - Liturgical language of Ethiopian Orthodox Church
  - Ethiopian Bible: 81 books (most complete canon)
  - Written in Ethiopic script (Fidel)
  - Triliteral root system (like Hebrew and Arabic)

Pronunciation Guide:
  አ (ʾ) = glottal stop or silent
  ኀ (ḫ) = voiceless pharyngeal fricative
  ዐ (ʿ) = voiced pharyngeal fricative
  ጸ (ṣ) = emphatic s
  ፀ (ḍ) = emphatic ts
  ጰ (ṗ) = emphatic p
